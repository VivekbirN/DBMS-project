Our Story


Taco Bell was born from the culinary dreams of three 4th semester students who wanted to create a unique dining experience that combines their favorite cuisines.

Our restaurant brings together the vibrant flavors of Mexican tacos, the comforting tradition of Italian pizzas, and the rich aroma of specialty coffee - all under one roof.

We're proud to offer vegetarian-friendly options across our menu, ensuring everyone can enjoy our fusion creations.

What are your operating hours?

Do you offer home delivery?

Yes, we offer home delivery within a 5km radius. Delivery is free for orders above ₹500.

Absolutely! We have a wide range of vegetarian options across all our menu categories.
